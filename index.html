Some random guinea pig facts

Guinea pigs do not originate from guinea, but are found in various different parts of the world, such in the andes of south america.

Guinea pig teeth never stop growing with their molars constantly growing throughout their life. To help them from growing too much they constantly chew on to wear down their teeth to prevent any painful dental problems.

Guinea pig are highly social animals that lives in herds for protection from other predators and communicate with each other through a variety of sounds.

Guinea pigs do not produce vitamin C like humans do. They lack the necessary enzyme to synthesize vitamin C, but instead recieve this essential netrient from a daily diet of rick vegetables like bell peppers and leafy greens or else they could potentially develop scurvy.
